Effective Date: April 2024

Introduction

Askwise.me ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

By using the AskWise.me extension, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our extension.

Information We Collect

To ensure the proper functioning and optimization of our services, we may collect the following information:

Necessary Information

  • Basic Device Information: This includes your browser type, plugin version, and the timestamp of your first activation. This information is essential for providing the service.
  • User-Generated Content: Metadata from the prompt templates you create, such as titles, tags, and usage scenario descriptions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Sensitive Data: We do not collect sensitive data such as your web browsing history, page text content, form input information, or social media account details.
  • Personal Identifiable Information: Unless you actively provide it, we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal contact information.
  • Financial Information: We do not collect your credit card details or bank account information.
  • Location Information: We do not collect your location information without informing you.

Use of Data

We process the collected information as follows:

  • Transmission and Storage: All data is transmitted using AES-256 encryption and stored in data centers located in the EU (Frankfurt) and North America (Oregon).
  • Management of User Templates: Template data is stored independently, not used for AI model training, and usage traces are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Third-Party Cooperation: We work with third-party service providers (e.g., AWS/GCP) who must pass annual security audits and are prohibited from using data for advertising or resale.
  • Service Improvement: We use collected data to operate, maintain, and improve AskWise extension functionality, including providing intelligent search suggestions and content analysis.
  • Personalized Experience: We use your usage data to personalize your experience, such as providing more relevant search results and suggestions.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Deletion: You can download your data package via the control panel or submit a deletion request (processed within 10 business days).
  • Correction: You have the right to edit the content of submitted templates.
  • Opt-Out of Personalization: You can disable the "Template Optimization Suggestions" feature to stop related data analysis.
  • Right to Object: In certain cases, you have the right to object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: In certain cases, you have the right to request restriction of processing your personal information.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transmit that information to another data controller.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Additionally, you can control the data we collect through the following methods:

  • Disabling or uninstalling our extension
  • Adjusting your browser's privacy settings
  • Clearing your browser cache and cookies

Security Measures

We implement the following measures to ensure data security:

  • Encryption Technology: Core algorithms are protected by Intel SGX technology.
  • Security Audits: We conduct regular internal and third-party security audits.
  • Data Encryption: We use standard encryption technologies to protect data transmission and storage.
  • Access Control: We restrict employee access to user data, and only employees who need to process information can access your data.
  • Data Retention: We only retain your information for as long as necessary or as required by law.

Despite these protective measures, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our extension may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect certain information. Cookies are small data files stored in your browser that allow us to remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience.

The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of the extension and cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how the extension is used so we can improve functionality and performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow the extension to remember your choices and preferences to provide a more personalized experience.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the extension.

When we transfer your personal information to other countries, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information and ensure data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
